For the People!

Nando's began as an opportunity to spread light and love every Sunday to our small community. We now aim to bring extra happiness and overall positive energy to whomever we interact with. We love donuts, we love granola, but our passion is people!

The Nando's Nuthouse just so happens to make the best donuts in Chapel Hill, and the best granola in the world. You won't find any other place quite as nutty as us!

A major portion of proceeds goes to:
Operation Underground Railroad.
